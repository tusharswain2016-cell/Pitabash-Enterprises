Who We Are

Creating Safer, Smarter Spaces For Children Since 2021

Pitabash Enterprises Private Limited was founded with a simple belief: every child deserves a space that is safe to explore, creative enough to inspire, and built to last. What started as a small initiative in Odisha has grown into a trusted name in child-friendly infrastructure.

We work hand-in-hand with government bodies, schools, crèches, and private families to design and install indoor play zones, outdoor playgrounds, and educational spaces — using premium, child-safe materials at every stage.

From the first concept sketch to final installation and ongoing maintenance, our team stays involved throughout, because a play space is only as good as the care that goes into building it.
