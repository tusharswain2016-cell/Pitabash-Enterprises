About Pitabash Enterprises

Since 2021, Pitabash Enterprises has been delivering innovative child care products and infrastructure solutions that combine safety, quality, and creativity. Our experienced team works closely with government bodies, educational institutions, corporate organizations, and private clients to create environments where children can learn, play, and thrive.

✓ End-to-End Project Execution
✓ Premium Child-Safe Materials
✓ Customized Designs for Every Space
✓ Trusted by Government & Educational Institutions

Our Mission

To design and deliver safe, creative, and child-friendly infrastructure that empowers every child to learn, play, and grow — while giving schools, institutions, and families complete peace of mind through quality materials and expert execution.

Our Vision

To become Odisha's most trusted name in child care infrastructure, setting the standard for safety and innovation in every playground, crèche, and educational space we build across the region.
